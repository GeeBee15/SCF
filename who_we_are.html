SheepGate Christian Fellowship International Ministries Inc.

About Us

SheepGate Christian Fellowship International Ministries Inc. is a member of the Philippine Evangelical Holiness Churches and were founded in October 2007 by a visionary group of families who shared a common dream: to establish a church that preaches the gospel and embodies the spirit of religious freedom rooted in the Evangelistic tradition and approach. We are an evangelical congregation with 100 total members and growing, including approximately 50 active core volunteers currently residing in the city of San Pedro Laguna, Philippines.

We envisioned a place of faithful and holy Church of God, comprised of mature disciples of Jesus Christ, where people have the right to interpret scripture for themselves, and where congregants can serve as priests to one another.

The cornerstone of our ministry has always been to glorify God by fulfilling the Great Commandment and the Great Commission.

Mission

To glorify God by fulfilling the great commandment and the great commission

Vision

A faithful and holy church of God compose of matured disciples of Jesus Christ
